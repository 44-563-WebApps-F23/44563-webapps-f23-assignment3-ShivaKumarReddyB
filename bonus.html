Boruto

Boruto is a character known for his extraordinary profession and unique personality traits.

Character Traits:

  • Intelligent
  • Resourceful
  • Adventurous

Profession: Surgeon

Boruto, commonly referred to as Dr. Uzumaki , is a renowned surgeon known for his exceptional skills in the operating room. He has saved countless lives and earned a reputation for his precision and dedication to his profession.
